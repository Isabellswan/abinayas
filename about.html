About

I am an Electrical and Electronics Engineering graduate from PSG College of Technology, currently developing a dual-track profile in space engineering and theoretical physics.

My work focuses on understanding space systems from both an engineering and scientific perspective, combining technical implementation with fundamental research.

I am particularly interested in cosmology, star formation, and the physical processes governing astrophysical systems, while also building expertise in power systems, control systems, and space-oriented engineering.

Through structured learning, independent projects, and research-oriented thinking, I am working toward a career in space exploration, with the long-term goal of contributing as both a space scientist and astronaut.
